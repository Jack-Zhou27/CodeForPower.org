Linear/Sequential Search
A linear search, also known as a sequential search, is pretty easy. Loop through the entire array until you find your target.



You can probably tell how straightforward linear searches are. Just use a for loop that loops through the entire array, while checking each value for the target. However, linear searches aren't really efficient. Even though computers can solve things really fast, doing linear searches for arrays sized 10^20 (which is pretty realistic in real-world applications), will probably time-out, as this array is trillions upon trillions in length.
